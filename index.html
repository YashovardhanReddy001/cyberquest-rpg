Cybercrime Awareness & Prevention
This game helps you recognise common cyber crimes and practise what to do in real life.
  • 🎣 Phishing: fake messages and links that steal passwords/money
  • 👾 Malware: harmful downloads and fake “update” popups
  • 👹 Scams: urgency, fear, and authority pressure tactics
  • 🎭 Impersonation: deepfakes and “someone you trust” requests
Take your time. The goal is learning, not speed.
Main Story

You are Alex Cipher, a newly recruited Cyber Defender still in training at the elite organization called “NetGuardians”.

A powerful cyber-criminal syndicate called “The Void Collective”, led by the mysterious hacker known as Nexus, launches a massive multi-stage attack on the digital world.

During the attack on your academy (training center), you survive a sophisticated phishing attack orchestrated by Nexus himself. However, your girlfriend Lira gets infected with a dangerous malware.

The only way to save her is to enter the Digital Nexus — a virtual representation of the internet structured as a multi-layered labyrinth. Each layer represents a major cyber threat. You must fight through these layers, reach the core, defeat Nexus, and obtain the Cure Key to save Lira.
