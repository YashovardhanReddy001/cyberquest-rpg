Cybercrime Awareness & Prevention
This game helps you recognise common cyber crimes and practise what to do in real life.
  • 🎣 Phishing: fake messages and links that steal passwords/money
  • 👾 Malware: harmful downloads and fake “update” popups
  • 👹 Scams: urgency, fear, and authority pressure tactics
  • 🎭 Impersonation: deepfakes and “someone you trust” requests
Take your time. The goal is learning, not speed.
Main Story

You are Alex Cipher, a newly recruited Cyber Defender still in training at the elite organization called “NetGuardians”.

A powerful cyber-criminal syndicate called “The Void Collective”, led by the mysterious hacker known as Nexus, launches a massive multi-stage attack on the digital world.

During the attack on your academy (training center), you survive a sophisticated phishing attack orchestrated by Nexus himself. However, your girlfriend Lira gets infected with a dangerous malware.

The only way to save her is to enter the Digital Nexus — a virtual representation of the internet structured as a multi-layered labyrinth. Each layer represents a major cyber threat. You must fight through these layers, reach the core, defeat Nexus, and obtain the Cure Key to save Lira.

    The Battle for Lira's Mind

    Backstory: The virtual domain has been corrupted by 'The Void Collective'. They have infected Lira, a senior NetGuardian, with malicious malware and lock-out codes. Her system is shut down, but her core is still transmitting. She is running out of time!

    Your Mission: As Alex Cipher, you must brave the three 3D labyrinth layers to recover the decryption codes. Each layer corresponds to critical threat vectors (Phishing, Scams, or Mixed Threats).

    Objectives:

    • Navigate and Cleanse: Move through the maze corridor by corridor. Defeat cyber threat beasts (phishing bots and scammers) guarding the paths.
    • Inspect evidence: Examine the forensic caches on each floor to gain threat intelligence.
    • Acquire Keys: Search every nook and cranny to find hidden security keys.
    • Set Passwords: Put your security training to work! Apply long, complex, and unique passwords to configure the keys.
    • Unlock Security Barriers: Open firewall gates with your configured passwords to advance to the next layer.
    • Save Lira: Clear the final floor, locate the ultimate Cure Key, and upload the patch to Lira before her system goes dark!

    Keyboard Controls

    • W / S (or Up / Down): Walk Forward / Backward
    • L / R: Turn Look Angle Left / Right
    • Left / Right Arrows: Strafe Side-to-Side
    • A (or Left Arrow in combat): Strike / Attack Cyber Threat
    • D (or Right Arrow in combat): Quick Dodge Enemy Strike
    • E / X / Enter: Interact (Pick up keys, inspect caches, open doors, climb ladders)
    • M: Toggle minimap view expansion
    • P: Access phone / Toggle SMS app 📱
    • H: Pause and open Help overlay directly
    • Escape: Pause/Resume game

    Controller Controls

    • Left Stick: Walk forward/backward, strafe left/right
    • Right Stick: Look / turn camera
    • A Button: Interact (collect keys, open doors, answer questions)
    • B Button: Go back / dismiss popup
    • Y Button: Open Phone
    • Start Button: Open Settings
    • Menu / Select Button: Open Pause Menu
